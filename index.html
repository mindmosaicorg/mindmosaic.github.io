Mind Mosaic: Recent Breakthroughs

Mind Mosaic is an Alexa skill that shares one recent scientific or technological breakthrough every time you ask. From AI to space exploration, quantum physics to medical discoveries — each insight is curated to spark wonder and lifelong learning.

How to Use It

  • Say: “Alexa, open Mind Mosaic”
  • Then: “Tell me a breakthrough” or “Next”
